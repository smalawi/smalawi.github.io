February 1 2022. Tuesday.

    Just when my calluses have gotten pretty good, I've gone two days without practicing. Did a lot of errands today and hopefully got ahead of enough of them to spread my time around a bit more for the rest of the week.

    I need to reevaluate this space, I think. I definitely prefer this medium to keeping a paper journal – adding images and screwing with the formatting and avoiding confrontation with my own handwriting are all good things. But I think it's falling back into the same pitfalls too: days of nothing worth recording, having to take time out of every day to write, going through the motions of recording events without much thought or substance. Pivoting to a blog type thing would also make more sense for something public-facing. But what would I blog about? Have to strike a balance somehow.
