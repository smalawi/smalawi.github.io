January 21 2022. Friday.

    Today's my last day in Florida. I'm flying back to New York with Jenna (my sister) and her cat tomorrow. I'd meant to drop by the library to get a card (in order to stop leeching off of my dad's Kanopy access) but there was actual work to be done and the day slipped away. I stepped out for a walk around six, heading first for this grassy expanse at the edge of the property, a few leftover acres between the swamp and the single-family houses. It was weirdly quiet, as golden hours go - aside from the crickets, nothing to be heard, save for a grackle hiccuping hoarsely to itself somewhere in the woods. Occasionally a pale little moth would fly from my path, skittering over the grass, too tremulous to get more than an inch or two off the ground. I spent a little time under a big evergreen of some sort, checking the pinecones for seeds the birds and squirrels had missed.
